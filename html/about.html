ABOUT ME



    

Deborah Buzuayehu is a New York based graphic designer. Buzuayehu started as a fine artist, but developed an interest for graphic design at an early age. She later enrolled in the pristegious design university Parsons School of Design for her undergraduate studies. Today Buzuayehu is a full time student who has managed to already gain small press from large design houses like Marc Jacobs. Her experience and study in design and business management provide solid support in the communication design department. Buzuayehus’ record of being rehired as an intern attest to her hard work and enthusiasm towards the future. She has found that her primary interest is in the advertising and brand design field.In the future, Buzuayehu hopes to use her design to create change and have a voice towards modern day issues. She is determined to use her skills as a means to reveal new perspectives, thereby improving the way we live our lives.
